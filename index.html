How we do it

An extensive support system for students offering assistance in the admission process to the top universities

STEP : 1

Profile Analysis

We will analyze your profile from the details which you provide for a few questions from us which will help us to understand your profile, strengths, limitations, goals etc.

STEP : 2

Essay Preperation 

Once the universities are finalized, we will discuss the essays and start working on the essays focusing on the content and lucidity and ensure those are distinct to your profile.

STEP : 3 

Resume Building

Simultaneously, we will work together on your resume which is integral to the application process, especially during the interviews.

         STEP : 4 

Letter Of Recommendation 

The Letter of Recommendation would be prepared as per the format suggested by the universities of interest, which again would be unique for the profile of each applicant. 

      STEP : 5 

Interview Cracking Tips

We will also support you to succeed in the interviews by conducting mock interviews and sharing a set of possible general questions so that you are equipped to handle the panel and can confidently answer any questions thrown at you.
